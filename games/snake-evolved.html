Snake Evolved

This game takes a spin from the original snake game, and makes it work with player perspective! This is similar to slither.io in its gameplay.

The main objective is to eat the randomly-spawning food pellets, which grows your snake. Try not to run into other players or the walls!

How to play:

Controls:

  • Player 1: W = boost, A = turn left, D = turn right
  • Player 2: Up Arrow = boost, Left Arrow = turn left, Right Arrow = turn right
  • Pause: Escape/Esc

FYI, the arrow acts like a compass, pointing north.

Keep in mind that this game does not (yet) support mobile devices. If you are on a mobile device right now, please switch to a computer to play this.
