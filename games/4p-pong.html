4-Player Pong

4-Player (multiplayer) Pong is a new spin on the classic Pong game! it now features the compatibility for 4 people to play at the same time!

How to play:

The Red Team (Players 1 and 2) duels against the Green Team (players 3 and 4)!

Controls:

  • Player 1 Move: Q = up, A = down
  • Player 2 Move: R = up, F = down
  • Player 3 Move: U = up, J = down
  • Player 4 Move: P = up, semicolon (;) = down
  • Pause: Space

Keep in mind that this game does not support mobile devices. If you are on a mobile device right now, please switch to a computer to play this.
