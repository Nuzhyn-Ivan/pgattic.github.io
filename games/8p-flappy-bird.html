8-Player Flappy Bird

Check this out! It's 8 players using one keyboard! I think you all have heard of the game that swept the nation many a year ago, known as Flappy Bird. Well, how about playing that game, but with an absurd amount of players in it? Presenting 8-Player Flappy Bird.

How to play:

Everybody gets one key that they press to "flap"

Controls:

  • Player 1 Flap: 1
  • Player 2 Flap: 2
  • Player 3 Flap: 3
  • Player 4 Flap: 4
  • Player 5 Flap: 5
  • Player 6 Flap: 6
  • Player 7 Flap: 7
  • Player 8 Flap: 8
  • Pause: Space

Keep in mind that this game does not support mobile devices. If you are on a mobile device right now, please switch to a computer to play this.
