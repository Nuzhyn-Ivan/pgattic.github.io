Yeet Ball Classic

This is the old version of Yeet Ball, kept alive for those who enjoyed it better than the new one.

A recent update introduces the power-up! Collect these rare blue squares to give you either one more life or one more Super Ability!

The Super Ability is a power that instantly kills any enemy on the screen. You start off with only three Super Ability uses, so use them wisely!

How to play:

Controls:

  • Move: Arrow Keys or WASD
  • Pause: Q
  • Use Super Ability: E
  • Shoot: Mouse

Keep in mind that this game does not (yet) support mobile devices. If you are on a mobile device right now, please switch to a computer to play this.
