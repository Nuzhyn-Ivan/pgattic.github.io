Pong

This is an ideration of the classic pong game that we all know and love! It supports one or two payers.

How to play:

Controls:

  • Player 1 Move: Q = up, A = down
  • Player 2 Move: P = up, ; = down (only if you're playing the two-player version!)
  • Pause: Space

Keep in mind that this game does not (yet) support mobile devices. If you are on a mobile device right now, please switch to a computer to play this.
