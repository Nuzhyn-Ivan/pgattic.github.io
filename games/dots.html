Dots

How to play:

Use your cursor to move the player around. You can only bump into dots smaller than you, and this will make you grow. Hitting an enemy that is larger than you will kill you instead. How big can you grow?

Controls:

  • Move: Mouse
  • Pause: Space

Keep in mind that this game does not support mobile devices. If you are on a mobile device right now, please switch to a computer to play this.
