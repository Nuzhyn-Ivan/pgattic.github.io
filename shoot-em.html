A newer version of Shoot 'Em! but with a scrolling background.

How to play:

Controls:

  • Move: Arrow Keys or WASD
  • Pause: Q
  • Use Super Ability: E
  • Shoot: Mouse

You start off with three lives and three Super Ability uses, so use them wisely!
