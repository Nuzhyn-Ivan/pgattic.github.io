Development of New Challenge: Checkbox Doodle!

7 January 2019

Just coded a brand-new challenge, which I call Checkbox Doodle! Simply put, you are given a grid of checkboxes, and you can "draw" with them like you would on any drawing application, but instead of drawing a line, it checks the boxes that you "draw" over. You can erase by holding "Shift"!
